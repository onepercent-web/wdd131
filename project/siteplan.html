WDD 131 Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Improve Your Sound

Reason:

This name expresses that the knowledge of effectors and learn how to use their knobs can improve the sound of one's music.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help users understand the meaning and operation of knobs on music effectors. Users will also learn about the functions of different types of knobs and the history of effectors.

Scenarios

  • Home
    • What can I learn from this site?
    • What are effectors and their knobs?
  • About Knobs
    • How do I operate the knobs on an effector?
    • How does turning the knobs change the sound?
  • Knob Functions
    • What are the different types of knobs?
  • Effector History
    • What were the first effectors like?
