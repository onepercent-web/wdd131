What are effectors and their knobs?

Effector is a device used to add changes to the sound of electronic instruments such as guitars and synthesizers. By using an effector, you can add distortion to a guitar's sound or delay the sound of a synthesizer. Effectors can change the original sound, making it possible to transform it into a variety of different tones. This is fascinating because it brings and enhances creativity in music production, making effectors is a wonderful tool. A knob is a rotary control used to adjust the sound. By turning the knob, you can adjust the effect of the effector. The interesting thing about knobs is that even a slight turn can change the sound completely. Finding the perfect sound by turning the knobs is truly enjoyable.
